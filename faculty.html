Dear Faculty,

 

CSULB's University Census date (February 15) has passed and your updated class rosters are now available in MyCSULB Faculty Center .


CLASS ROSTERS:

  • Rosters reflect the final official enrollment for your class.
  • Your roster also lists students who withdrew after February 1 and were assigned a grade of W .
  • Students approved to audit will have Audit noted after their name.
  • Students not listed on your class roster cannot continue to attend and will not receive credit.
  • Your Grade Roster will be available May 8 with final grades due May 18.


PETITIONS TO ADD AFTER 2/15:

  • If you believe a student is not enrolled due to a technical error or a situation beyond the student's control, ( Policy Statement 85-05 ), refer the student to your department office, college, or Enrollment Services to obtain the Petition to Add a Class After the Deadline form.

 

WITHDRAWAL REQUESTS ARE ONLINE:

  • To approve or deny a student's withdrawal request, log into your MyCSULB Faculty Center and view your Worklist . For instructions, visit Reviewing Requests to Withdraw .
  • February 2 - April 14: Requests require approval of the instructor and department chair.
  • April 15 - May 5: Requests require approval of the instructor, department chair, and dean. Final approval can only be granted by the University Withdrawal Committee.
  • Review CSULB's University Withdrawal Policies .


FAMILY EDUCATIONAL RIGHTS AND PRIVACY ACT (FERPA):

  • All students' records, including grades, are protected by FERPA.
  • Please note that the public posting of grades, either by student name, CSULB ID number, social security number, or any other possible identifiers (or even partial identifiers) is a violation of FERPA. Faculty should NOT post grades in hallways, department offices, or alternative online channels (including email, websites and social media).
  • For more information, visit FERPA for Faculty .


Please contact Registration and Records via es-registration@csulb.edu if you have any questions. Best wishes for a successful semester!
